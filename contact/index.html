Frequently Asked Questions

How do you evaluate spaces for neuro-accessibility?

We use a comprehensive framework that assesses acoustic comfort, lighting, spatial design, sensory factors, and cognitive support features.

Can I suggest a space for evaluation?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from our community. Contact us with the space details and we'll consider it for evaluation.

Do you offer training for workspace providers?

Yes, we provide training and consultation services to help workspace providers improve their neuro-accessibility features.

How often are spaces re-evaluated?

We conduct regular reviews and updates based on user feedback and any changes to the workspace environment.
